Plant Simulator Built in C++ OpenGL

April - May 2020

For my final project in my Computer Graphics class, I built an interactive plant game, where you can plant seeds, water it, and watch it bloom!

What features does it have?

  • Particle system
  • Swept surfaces
  • Animation
  • Interactions via keyboard and mouse clicks
  • Lighting
  • Bezier curves
  • Bitmaps and textures
  • Clipping planes
