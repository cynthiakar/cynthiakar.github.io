recipE-Z: the recipe finder app

January - May 2020

What is recipE-Z?

As a way to help alleviate food waste, we built an iOS app that will present you with recipes based on the ingredients you already have. A user can enter a list of ingredients or a specific recipe and then our app will search through a database of recipes, from online or submitted by users in our app. It will present results ranging from recipes that can be made with everything the user already has to recipes that will require extra ingredients. The results can be filtered by meal and other options. Our target audience / market are people who are on budgets, such as students, but we believe this app will be useful for anyone.

What makes recipE-Z unique?

We want this app to be accessible to people with a wide range of income levels and include recipes from diverse cultures. Thus, in addition to searching through the Spoonacular API, which contains a database of recipes, we also wanted to create our own database for users to save their own recipes and share them with others in the app. Thus, we have a diverse set of user-generated and online recipes for users to search through.
