capstone

Resumate.co is your best mate when it comes to getting a job. For my senior capstone, my partner and I created a website to help guide teens and other newcomers through the job search process. To do this, we created a resume builder using only Javascript, HTML, and CSS for print. Users just fill out a form and the website will give them a page to print out. There is also an outfit guide featuring outfits for the various work environments. Lastly, there is an interview questions guide where users can practice answering questions and print out their responses to review before their interview.Click here to view the final project
